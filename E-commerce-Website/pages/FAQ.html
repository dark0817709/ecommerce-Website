Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about M.Store

What makes M.Store different from other marketplaces?

M.Store specializes in curated luxury items with a focus on exceptional quality and craftsmanship. Every product is handpicked by our expert team, ensuring you receive only the finest pieces that combine beauty, functionality, and lasting value.

How do you ensure product authenticity?

We work directly with verified artisans, designers, and authorized dealers. Each item comes with a certificate of authenticity, and our team conducts thorough quality inspections before any product reaches our customers.

What is your shipping and delivery policy?

We offer white-glove delivery service for all items. Delivery times vary by location and product, typically 7-14 business days. Large furniture pieces include professional setup and installation at no additional cost.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition. Custom or personalized pieces may have different return terms. Contact our concierge team for specific return procedures.

Do you offer financing options?

We partner with premium financing companies to offer flexible payment plans for qualified customers. Options include 6, 12, and 24-month terms with competitive rates for purchases over $1,000.

How can I become a seller on M.Store?

We carefully select our sellers based on quality, craftsmanship, and brand alignment. Click "Become a Seller" to submit your application. Our team reviews each application and will contact qualified candidates within 5-7 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to select international destinations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact our concierge team for specific international shipping information and customs requirements.
